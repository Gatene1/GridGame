Arrows: move • B: hold breath (wake toy) • Reach the EXIT

Reach the exit by holding your breath with 'B'
which activates a toy to press the switch
to let you cross the water to the exit.
Be careful and watch your 'Breath' and 'Cooldown' meters.
If you run out of breath, you’ll black out and restart the level.
